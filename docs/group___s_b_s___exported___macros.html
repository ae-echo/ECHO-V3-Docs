◆ __HAL_SBS_CLEAR_MEMORIES_ERASE_STATUS

#define __HAL_SBS_CLEAR_MEMORIES_ERASE_STATUS ( __FLAG__)
Value:

Clear SBS Memories Erase Status Flags.

Parameters
__FLAG__specifies the flag to clear. This parameter can be one of the following values:
  • SBS_MEMORIES_ERASE_FLAG_IPMEE Status of End Of Erase for ICACHE and PKA RAMs
  • SBS_MEMORIES_ERASE_FLAG_MCLR Status of Erase after Power-on Reset ((SRAM2, BKPRAM, ICACHE, DCACHE, PKA RAMs)
